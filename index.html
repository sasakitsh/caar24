Features

Multiviews and Multi devices

FixedCamera with multiview, Drone with Camera, GoPro. The fixed cameras are firming from four directions.

2.1K Videos and 1.0M Frames

Untrimmed videos with 3840x2160@30FPS. Annotate proper/improper actions for each video.

Pairs of proper and improper actions

For instance: Proper and improper use of the harness (attach it or not) when using an electric lift.

Industrial and everyday acitons

Industrial Actions such as height working with electrical lift, working with stepladder, suspended load etc.

Indoor and Outdoors

Filmed on the grounds and in the gymnasium, there are a wide variety of background and variations in lighting conditions.

9 Actors

For work at height or stepladder, attire is work clothes and helmets. For everyday scenes such as sports, a variety of attire.
